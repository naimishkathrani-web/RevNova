5-Phase Implementation Strategy

RevNova follows a phased approach to minimize risk and ensure successful migration:

PHASE 1 - MVP

Product Catalog (WS1)

Timeline: 3 months

Scope: Product2, Pricebook2, PricebookEntry migration with AI field mapping and validation framework

Deliverables: Migration wizard (7 pages), API framework, hybrid staging model (Vanilla + EAV)

PHASE 2

Quotes & Pricing (WS2)

Timeline: 2 months

Scope: SBQQ__Quote__c, QuoteLine, Discount Schedules with pricing transform logic

Deliverables: Quote builder integration, discount schedule mapping, batch pricing updates

PHASE 3

Contracts & Assets (WS3)

Timeline: 2 months

Scope: Subscription → Asset conversion, Contract lifecycle management

Deliverables: Asset tracking, contract term handling, renewal automation

PHASE 4

Billing & Invoicing (WS4)

Timeline: 2 months

Scope: Invoice generation, payment tracking, billing schedule automation

Deliverables: Billing account setup, invoice templates, payment reconciliation

PHASE 5

Revenue Cloud Advanced (WS5)

Timeline: 3 months

Scope: Product hierarchies, bundles, EPC integration, advanced pricing

Deliverables: Product catalog hierarchy, bundle configuration, EPC API integration
